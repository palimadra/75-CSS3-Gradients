A) CSS Files and Structure - top

All of the gradients are in the 75css3gradients.css stylesheet. I suggest taking the gradients that you want and putting them in your own styles rather than linking the 75css3gradients.css stylesheet.

The gradients are grouped by color but are not in order of color but rather in ranked by my favorites (meaning the most used by me).
